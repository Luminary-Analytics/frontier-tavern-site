The last warm hearth before the map runs out

You keep the only tavern on the frontier's edge of a magical land. Pour ale, stew what the valley gives you, and build a reputation good enough that travelers trust your roof for the night — because past the creek and the mountain pass, something pale waits for a warm bowl of glowing stew.

  • Cook & serve — five recipes, wood-fired stove, impatient regulars
  • Grow & gather — garden, forage, and fish the valley
  • Build & rearrange — move the bar, the kegs, even the stove
  • Rent rooms — four guest rooms upstairs, paid nightly
  • Trade with Ferris — the peddler buys, sells, and carries rumors
  • Day & night — sun and moon over a living frontier valley
